100 Days Of Coding: Day 1 Expanding Cards Project

There are 2 different approaches to learning -

  1. Learning many different skills just as much as necessary to get working knowledge about those different skill sets.
  2. Learning just one skill deeply and being an expert in it.

You can read about why I transitioned from the first type of learning to the second type here.

At the beginning of January 2023, I decided that I shall be learning just ONE thing consistently for 6 months and see how deep I could go about it.

After deciding that, I had to pick what I wanted to learn deeply for the first 6 months of 2023.

I have many interests but the 2 things that can keep me awake at night are - writing and coding. I am an amateur in both of these skills. That's why I decided that I will devote the whole of 2023 to mastering these skills.

In the first 6 months from January to June 2023, I'll learn coding. And the latter half of the year I'll master the art of digital writing.

So, here it is!
